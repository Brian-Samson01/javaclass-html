Geographic information systems

A geographical information system integrates hardware, software and data for capturing, managing, analyzing and displaying all forms of geographically referenced information

GIS is used to:

  1. map where things are
  2. map quantities
  3. map densities
  4. find what inside
  5. find what nearby
  6. map change
